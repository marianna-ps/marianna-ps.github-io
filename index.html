This is a random challenge generator for the game Phasmophobia. By pressing the challenge-button, you get a random challenge and the player(s) who are supposed to complete the challenge.

Feel to use this page in your streams and such. You can chroma key the background and have the page overlayed in your stream.

The page is not maintained regularly - it is just a small practise project :)

Start by adding players (one player at a time). You can add up to 4 players. You can add players after taking a challenge too - just add a new player and get a new challenge. Players can't be deleted - if you want a new game with different players, you have to refresh.
